La Charca tavern restaurant wants to offer their guests the concept of a traditional tavern, but with modern touches as well as vintage, a mixture that undoubtedly makes this place special.

It is located in Plaza España, and it is open from breakfast until diner and they offer traditional food elaborated with seasonal fresh products.
