It is located in the centre of Madrid, in the city's historic Plaza Mayor, close to the Puerta del Sol.

Gustos Madrid is in the south-east corner of the square, next to the Arco de Cuchilleros. It offers a great variety of dishes, particularly our speciality paellas and rich dishes, which you can watch as they are cooked in our outer kitchen.

It combines the tradition of fine rice in an original setting, outside on the Plaza Mayor terrace, or inside on either of the restaurant's two floors, where you can enjoy the experience of eating in their fully adapted caves with modern, welcoming decor for an absolutely unique experience.
